Contraception IQ

Contraception IQ makes it easy to use the CDC Medical Eligibility Criteria for Contraceptive Use to provide up-to-date, evidence-based contraception guidance for your patients.

Select Conditions

Select all of the conditions that apply to your patient. Contraception IQ includes conditions from the latest CDC Medical Eligibility Criteria for Contraceptive Use.

View Classifications

Contraception IQ calculates the classifications for each birth control method, based on the selected conditions. The classifications are displayed in a simple table – no need for complex wheels or charts.

Detailed Results

When viewing the classifications, tap on any birth control method to see how each condition contributed to the classification.
